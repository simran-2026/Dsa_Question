<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/stickler-theif-1587115621/1">Stickler Thief</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Medium</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO"><p data-start="182" data-end="427"><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">Stickler the thief wants to loot money from the houses arranged in a line. He cannot loot <strong>two consecutive</strong> houses and aims to <strong>maximize </strong>his total loot.<br></span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">Given an array, <strong>arr[]</strong> where arr[i] represents the amount of money in the i-th house. </span><span style="font-size: 18.6667px;">Determine the <strong>maximum </strong>amount he can loot.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>arr[] = [6, 7, 1, 3, 8, 2, 4]
<strong>Output: </strong>19
<strong>Explanation: </strong>Maximum amount he can get by looting 1st, 3rd, 5th and 7th house, which is 6 + 1 + 8 + 4 = 19.</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Input: </strong>arr[] = [5, 3, 4, 11, 2]
<strong>Output: </strong>16
<strong>Explanation: </strong>Maximum amount he can get by looting 1st and 4th house, which is 5 + 11 = 16.<br></span></pre>
